App Description:

The application is specific to a wine bar.

The Owner will add the wines in his bar and let customers write reviews and order wines.

Customers will be able to write and read their reviews that are persistent and can be filtered by type of wine.

Customers will be able to order x number of wines and receive and email confirmation of their purchase.
